Community

Shared Experiences

Neighbors compare notes, sharing stories of fleeting shadows, laughter in the empty streets, and the unsettling yet oddly performative nature of the encounters. Many describe a feeling of being observed—but not endangered.

Analysis

What the Witnesses Say

Recurring themes in the accounts: sudden appearances, precise timing, and an almost theatrical execution of each encounter. The streets after midnight have become a stage, and these witnesses the unintended audience.
